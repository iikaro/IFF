Find out more about the IFF Virtual tour

In 2020, throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, which requires social distancing measures, museums and galleries have recognized the need to adapt to the constantly changing artistic landscape. The Figueiredo Ferraz Institute (IFF), based in Ribeirão Preto, was no exception. Faced with this atypical context, the mechatronics engineer and master's student in Mechanical Engineering at the São Carlos Engineering School (EESC) at USP, Ícaro Ostan, in collaboration with the IFF art educator, Ingrid Assis Ostan, developed a gamified virtual visit.
